The race to reach net-zero by 2050 has sparked an explosion in "Climate Tech" innovation. Beyond simple solar and wind, we are now seeing the maturation of technologies that were considered science fiction only a decade ago.

From direct air capture (DAC) plants in Iceland that turn CO2 into stone to next-generation geothermal energy that taps into the heat of the Earth's crust regardless of location, the solution set for the climate crisis is expanding rapidly.

### What is leading the transition?
- **Battery Chemistries**: New developments in solid-state and sodium-ion batteries promise to solve the supply-chain issues of lithium while increasing safety and efficiency.
- **Green Hydrogen**: The "Swiss Army Knife" of decarbonization is finally becoming commercially viable as electrolyzer costs plummet.
- **Precision Agriculture**: AI-driven farming techniques are reducing fertilizer use by up to 90%, preserving soil health while maintaining yields.
