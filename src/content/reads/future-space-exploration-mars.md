Humanity's expansion into the stars is no longer just a thought experiment. The 2020s are set to be the "golden age" of robotic and crewed exploration as costs per kilogram to orbit have plummeted thanks to reusable rockets.

From the Artemis program's goal to establish a permanent lunar presence to the first test flights of massive Starship configurations, the pathway to Mars is clearer than ever.

**Key Mission Milestones:**
1. **Lunar Gateway**: An orbital station around the Moon that will serve as a hub for deep-space missions.
2. **Biosphere 3.0**: New experiments in closed-loop life support systems that could sustain humans on Mars for months without restocking.
3. **Europa Clipper**: Searching for the chemical signatures of life in the sub-surface oceans of Jupiter's moons.
